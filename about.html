About Us

Blarark VI is Lagos' premier destination for luxury dining and entertainment. Located at 14 Adeola Hopewell, Victoria Island, we offer an escape into a world of refined taste and exceptional service.

Our culinary team crafts dishes that celebrate both local Nigerian flavors and international cuisine, using only the finest ingredients. From our signature pepper soups to our carefully curated cocktail menu, every item is designed to delight.

By night, Blarark VI transforms into a vibrant social hub, featuring live music, DJ sets, and exclusive events. Our VIP tables offer an intimate experience with premium bottle service, while our main lounge buzzes with energy.

Whether you are joining us for a business lunch, celebrating a special occasion, or seeking the perfect night out, Blarark VI promises an experience beyond expectations.
